

PZG-Tools v2.0rc2 for Linux i386
from 22.Sep.2007


-------------
INSTALLATION
-------------

Just copy all files in the bin-directory
as root to /usr/local/bin and make
a link on your Desktop to 
/usr/local/bin/PZGTools2

Get sure that the files are executable
for all users.


-------------
REQUIREMENTS
-------------

Following has to be installed
on your system:

(1) Perl
(2) GnuPlot
(3) QWT/QT4-libraries
